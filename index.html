About Us

In our busy schedule fresh and homemade food preparation is difficult in early mornings or when we come back home late night. But it is made possible by Foodureca. Foodureca carry home like food and deliver the same before the meal time. This is a Web page application through which people of a Mess can order meal services online using the Internet. Now a day’s ready made food is easily available but homemade food and its taste is a different matter. This online service will not let you be deprived from the food that tastes like home made food which is just not healthy but also economical. Just a single click under your finger that will bring healthy and delicious food at your footsteps. The aim of our company is to have is to put our Team and their actual performance in the supply and management of food delivery service. This web application allows Mumbaikars to directly order a dabba for their afternoon lunch online. This application is built to be beneficial to student knowledge as well as help Mumbai dabbawala’s.
